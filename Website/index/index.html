Our Services

Check-up

A dental consultation is a professional meeting with a dentist to assess your oral health.

Cleaning

Oral prophylaxis is a dental cleaning procedure that removes plaque, tartar, and stains from the teeth.

Jacket Crown

A jacket crown is a type of dental crown that covers the entire tooth to restore its shape, size, and strength.

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is a procedure used to save a severely decayed or infected tooth.

Bunot

Tooth extraction is the removal of a tooth from its socket in the jawbone.

Pasta

Tooth restoration is a dental procedure that repairs or replaces damaged or missing tooth structure.
