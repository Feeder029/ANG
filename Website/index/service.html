Check-up

A dental consultation is a professional appointment with a dentist to evaluate your oral health and discuss any concerns or goals you may have about your teeth, gums, or smile.


During a dental consultation, the dentist may:


  • - Examine your teeth, gums, and mouth
  • - Take X-rays if needed
  • - Identify any dental issues (like cavities or gum disease)
  • - Recommend treatments or procedures
  • - Answer your questions about dental care or cosmetic options
